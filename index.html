ABOUT US

First impressions are very important! That is why we want you to know all about BG's Engine Clinic!

With 30 years' experience in Outdoor Power Equipment Service and Repair, we bring a long tradition of quality repair service to your lawn & garden equipment.

Brian started working on lawn mowers at the age of 12, and a couple years later, established BG's Engine Clinic (1983) while still in High School. We've been working with Power Equipment ever since!

Since then, Brian has been an ASE Certified Triple Master mechanic (Master Truck, Master Auto, Master Machinist, with Advanced L1 and L2 electronics certifications), Ford Factory trained Technician (nearly achieved Ford Master Engine Technician), Heavy Equipment and Heavy truck mechanic, PA State certified Inspection Mechanic, and most recently, a 5 year stint as a programmer analyst and Linux systems administrator for a Web-Hosting company in Miami, Florida. However, our heart has always been in Lawn & Garden service and repairs, so, We are back and ready to build an even better small engine shop than we had before!

We are currently an Authorized servicing dealer for Echo, Shindaiwa, BearCat, Kero-World wicks, parts & accessories, Desa barrel heaters, Mr, Heater heaters, Jiffy Ice Drills, Badger Earth Drills, Rapala Strikemaster Ice Augers, Briggs & Stratton products, Kohler, Tecumseh, Honda Engines, MTD Products including Columbia, Troy-Bilt, Yardman, Yard Machines and White Outdoor brands.

We also carry B3C Fuel Solutions products and highly recommend their use to our customers.

We sell our products on many sales venues, including here, Amazon and on eBay, with thousands of products in stock and ready to ship.

HOW TO FIND US

Convenient location at the corner of Main & Pomeroy St. in Roulette, PA

From Coudersport: Take U.S. Route 6 West to Roulette.

If taking first left into Roulette: travel west on Main Street through town, past the post office and town park, Shop will be on your left before the second 4-way intersection.

If taking the Second left into Roulette (just before Close's lumber and the Baseball diamond), travel south on Fishing Creek Extension to the stop sign, turn left and shop is immediately on the right.

From Port Allegany: Take U.S. Route 6 East to Roulette, Take the SECOND right turn into town, just past Close's Lumber and the Baseball diamond, follow Fishing Creek Extension south to the stop sign, turn left, shop is immediately on the right.
